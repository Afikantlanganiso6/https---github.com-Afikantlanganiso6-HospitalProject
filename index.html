Services

Emergency Services

Immediate treatment for acute illnesses and injuries. Available 24/7 for life-threatening conditions requiring urgent medical attention..

Surgical Services

Comprehensive care for patients undergoing surgical procedures. Includes pre-operative, operative, and post-operative care.

Maternity Services

Care for women during pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum. Includes prenatal visits, labor and delivery support, and neonatal care.

Radiology Services

Diagnostic imaging services such as X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds. Used to diagnose and monitor various medical conditions.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Specialized care for critically ill patients requiring close monitoring and advanced medical support. Equipped with advanced life-support systems.

Outpatient Services

Medical treatments and consultations that do not require an overnight stay. Includes routine check-ups, minor surgeries, and specialist consultations.
